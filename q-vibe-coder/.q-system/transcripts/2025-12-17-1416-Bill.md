### Major Design Pivot - "The Learning Commons"
User explained critical insight from lost session:
> "The live algorithmic feeds will have as much or more learning opportunities than the live sessions we host. So the feeds are critical to the entire site."

This changes everything - feeds ARE the learning, not just social features.

### Critique of Twitter/X for Learning
Identified problems with Twitter/X model for education:
- Infinite scroll optimizes for addiction, not understanding
- Chronological chaos - no sense of progression
- Shallow engagement - like and move on
- Everything looks the same - tips look like jokes
- Ephemeral - good insights disappear
- No "finished" state - never caught up

### "Learning Commons" Concept Development
Designed new approach from first principles:
- **Finite, not infinite** - Clear "You're caught up!" state
- **Typed content** - Tips, Questions, Challenges, Wins (each looks different)
- **Active actions** - "Got it ‚úì" marks understanding, not just "like"
- **Course context** - Every card shows which course it's from
- **Journey visibility** - Always see Learn ‚Üí Certify ‚Üí Teach ‚Üí Earn progress
- **Peer awareness** - See who's learning alongside you
- **Intentional saving** - Build personal knowledge library
